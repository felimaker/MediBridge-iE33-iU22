Mastery of Generations
A through G

The iE33/iU22 platform evolved significantly from 2008 to 2012. We understand the specific board revisions, power requirements, and software baselines for each distinct "Cart".

Why this matters?

Mixing parts from different generations (e.g., putting a D-cart UMB in an F-cart) causes system instability. We verify every part number.

A-Series

2008

The foundation of the unified platform architecture.

B & C Series

2008-2009

Workflow optimizations and initial stabilization of the vision processing.

D & E Series

2010

Major firmware updates, enhanced hardware reliability, and new probe support.

F-Series (xMATRIX)

2011

The technological leap. Real-time 4D, xMatrix transducers, and superior processing power.

G-Series

2012

The final and most stable revision of the classic iE33/iU22 architecture.
